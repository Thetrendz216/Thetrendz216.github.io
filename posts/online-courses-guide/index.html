How to Create and Sell Online Courses in 2026

April 20, 2026 · 2 min

Introduction#

Creating and selling online courses is one of the most profitable things you can do in 2026. If you have any knowledge or skill, you can package it into a course and sell it to students worldwide.

Why Online Courses Are So Profitable#

  • Create once and sell forever
  • No physical products needed
  • Students from anywhere in the world
  • Earn while you sleep
  • Build authority in your field

What Can You Teach?#

You don’t need to be a university professor! People pay to learn skills like:

  • How to cook specific dishes
  • How to speak a new language
  • How to use software programs
  • How to start a business
  • How to play a musical instrument
  • Photography and videography
  • Fitness and health coaching

Step 1: Choose Your Course Topic#

Pick something you know well that others want to learn. Research what people are searching for and what problems they need solved. Validate your idea before creating.

Step 2: Plan Your Course Content#

Structure your course with:

  • A clear learning outcome
  • Module by module breakdown
  • Video lessons for each topic
  • Assignments and quizzes
  • A certificate of completion

Step 3: Record Your Course#

You don’t need expensive equipment:

  • Use your smartphone camera
  • Record in a quiet bright room
  • Use free editing software like DaVinci
  • Create slides with Canva for free

Step 4: Choose Your Platform#

Best platforms to sell courses:

  • Udemy — Huge marketplace with built in audience
  • Teachable — Great for your own brand
  • Gumroad — Simple and beginner friendly
  • Selar — Perfect for African creators

Step 5: Price Your Course#

Research similar courses and price accordingly:

  • Mini course: $10-$30
  • Standard course: $30-$100
  • Premium course: $100-$500

Step 6: Market Your Course#

  • Share on all social media platforms
  • Create free YouTube content to attract students
  • Build an email list of interested people
  • Partner with influencers in your niche
  • Offer limited time discounts to boost sales

How Much Can You Earn?#

A course selling for $50 with 100 students earns you $5000. Sell to 1000 students and you earn $50,000! The potential is truly unlimited with the right topic.

Conclusion#

Creating an online course is one of the best investments of time you can make. The work happens once but the income keeps coming for years. Start planning your first course today!
