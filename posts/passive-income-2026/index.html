5 Passive Income Ideas for 2026

April 19, 2026 · 2 min

Introduction#

Passive income means earning money while you sleep! Here are 5 ways to do it.

1. Blogging#

Write posts once and earn ad money forever from traffic.

2. YouTube Channel#

Upload videos and earn from ads every time someone watches.

3. Sell Digital Products#

Create ebooks or templates and sell them over and over again.

4. Stock Photography#

Upload your photos to Shutterstock and earn every time someone downloads them.

5. Affiliate Marketing#

Promote products and earn commission every time someone buys through your link.

Conclusion#

Passive income takes time to build but pays forever. Start today!
