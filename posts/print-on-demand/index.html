How to Start a Print on Demand Business in 2026

April 20, 2026 · 2 min

Introduction#

Print on demand is one of the easiest online businesses to start in 2026 with zero upfront investment. You design products, a company prints and ships them for you and you keep the profit. No stock, no shipping, no hassle!

What is Print on Demand?#

Print on demand allows you to sell custom designed products like t-shirts, mugs, phone cases and more. When a customer orders, the print on demand company produces and ships it automatically. You never touch the product!

Best Products to Sell#

Most popular print on demand products:

  • T-shirts and hoodies
  • Coffee mugs and water bottles
  • Phone cases
  • Tote bags and backpacks
  • Posters and wall art
  • Pillows and blankets

Best Print on Demand Platforms#

Printful#

Most popular platform with high quality products and worldwide shipping to over 200 countries including Africa.

Printify#

Cheaper prices with many print providers to choose from. Great for maximizing profit.

Redbubble#

Has its own marketplace so customers find you without needing your own store.

Merch by Amazon#

Sell directly on Amazon to their millions of daily shoppers. Highly competitive but extremely profitable.

Step 1: Choose Your Niche#

Don’t try to appeal to everyone. Pick a specific audience like:

  • Dog lovers
  • Gym enthusiasts
  • Teachers and educators
  • Gaming fans
  • Country or cultural pride designs

Step 2: Create Your Designs#

You don’t need to be a professional artist! Free design tools include:

  • Canva — Very easy to use
  • Adobe Express — Professional results
  • Kittl — Great for t-shirt designs

Step 3: Set Up Your Store#

Connect your print on demand platform to:

  • Shopify store
  • Etsy marketplace
  • Amazon Merch
  • Your own website

Step 4: Price for Profit#

Always calculate your profit margin:

  • T-shirt production cost: $10-$15
  • Your selling price: $25-$35
  • Your profit: $10-$20 per shirt

Step 5: Market Your Products#

  • Create TikTok videos showing designs
  • Post on Instagram and Pinterest
  • Run Facebook ads to your store
  • Partner with influencers in your niche

How Much Can You Earn?#

Beginners earn $200-$1000 per month. Established sellers earn $2000-$10000. Top sellers earn $20,000+ per month.

Conclusion#

Print on demand is a real business with real potential. The barrier to entry is low but the rewards can be massive. Start designing your first products today and build your passive income empire!
