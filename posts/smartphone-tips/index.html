10 Hidden Smartphone Features You Need to Know in 2026

April 19, 2026 · 2 min

Introduction#

Your smartphone is more powerful than you think! Most people only use 20% of their phone features. In this post we reveal 10 hidden features that will change how you use your phone every day.

1. Screen Recording#

Did you know your phone can record your screen? This is perfect for tutorials, saving video calls and sharing gameplay. On Android go to Quick Settings and look for Screen Recorder.

2. One Handed Mode#

Struggling to use your big phone with one hand? Most phones have a one handed mode. On Samsung swipe down on the home button to shrink the screen.

3. Guest Mode#

Let someone borrow your phone without seeing your personal data. Guest mode creates a separate profile with no access to your photos, messages or apps.

4. Quick Launch Camera#

Never miss a photo moment again! Double press the power button to instantly open your camera from any screen.

5. Built in File Scanner#

You don’t need a separate scanning app. Most phones can scan documents using the camera. Look for a document scanner in your camera or Google Drive app.

6. Smart Battery Saver#

Extend your battery life by enabling adaptive battery mode. Your phone learns which apps you use most and limits power to background apps automatically.

7. Back Tap Feature#

On many phones you can assign actions to tapping the back of your phone. Set it to take screenshots, open apps or control your media player.

8. Live Translation#

Point your camera at text in any language and your phone translates it instantly! This works in Google Translate and is incredibly useful when traveling.

9. Scheduled Messages#

Did you know you can schedule messages to send at a specific time? Perfect for sending birthday messages, reminders and business communications.

10. Digital Wellbeing Tools#

Track exactly how much time you spend on each app. Set daily limits so you don’t waste too much time on social media. Find it in your phone settings.

Conclusion#

Your smartphone is an incredibly powerful tool. Learning these hidden features will save you time, improve your productivity and make your life easier every day!
